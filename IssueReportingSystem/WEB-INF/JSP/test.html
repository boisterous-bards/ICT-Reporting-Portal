Education

CourseDatesLocationDescription
Quality Teaching Intensive Program (QTIP) Conducted on request TBA Designed to help teachers gain authentic conceptual understandings of the Quality Teaching framework and its tools, as well as developing an action plan and implementation process for integrating the Quality Teaching framework within participating Schools. Contact Professor Jenny Gore for more information.
Rock and Water Conducted on request TBA Run through our Family Action Centre, the Rock & Water program offers teachers a new way to interact with boys and girls through physicalsocial teaching. It leads from simple self-defence and boundary and communication exercises to a strong notion of self-confidence.
Teachers' Visit Day Annually, mid-year Callaghan campus, Newcastle Academic staff of the University lead sessions that provide teachers in Hunter and Central Coast schools with cutting edge teaching research, information relating to subject disciplines, and fresh approaches to teaching and learning, including advances in technology. This is run annually between June and July. Contact Tanille Elley for more information.
